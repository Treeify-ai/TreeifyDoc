## Summary

Treeify is built to reshape how test cases are designed, offering a faster, smarter, and more efficient way to work. With AI, you can simplify the test design process, improve team collaboration, and produce higher-quality test case output that better fits real business needs.

Your feedback matters to us as we continue refining and improving Treeify. If you need help while using the product, or if you have suggestions, feel free to contact us at any time.
