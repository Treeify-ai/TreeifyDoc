## General Questions

### Q1. What is Treeify?

Treeify is an AI-driven test design and test case generation tool built to help teams produce test cases with higher efficiency and more stable quality. Treeify presents generated results as an **editable mind map**, making it easier for QA teams to inspect structure, review coverage, and revise results iteratively.

### Q2. Can I use Treeify without uploading files?

Yes. You can manually enter requirement information directly into the text input area on the page. Treeify can then generate test objects and test scenarios based on your description.

### Q3. Does Treeify offer a free trial?

Yes. During the current beta phase, each account receives 100 free Credits.

---

## Files and Data

### Q4. What types of files can I upload?

You can upload files that contain business requirements, system design details, user stories, and related content, including Word documents, PDFs, spreadsheets, and similar materials.

### Q5. Is my uploaded data secure?

Yes. All uploaded files are encrypted during transmission and stored securely. Treeify does not retain your data beyond the period required for processing.

---

## Editing and Export

### Q6. Can I edit the generated test cases?

Yes. You can review and edit any generated content either through the mind map editor or through the detailed editing form provided on the page.

### Q7. Which export formats are supported?

The currently supported export formats are:

- Excel
- JSON
