---
title: 4.1 How Treeify Credits Work
---

Treeify uses a Credits-based pricing model to support its AI test design capabilities as a usage-based resource. Each generation action consumes a certain number of Credits based on task complexity.

---

## Which actions consume Credits?

Credits are consumed when you perform any of the following actions:

- **Requirement Analysis**
- **Test Object Generation**
- **Test Scenario Generation**
- **Re-generating any step**
- **AI Chat**

Each of these actions triggers Treeify's multi-agent reasoning workflow. The more complex the input and the more details it contains, the more Credits may be consumed.

---

## What affects Credit usage?

Credit consumption is calculated dynamically and is typically influenced by:

- The **length** and **level of detail** in the requirement content
- The number of testing dimensions involved, such as functional, security, or performance
- The **depth of token usage** during multi-agent LLM analysis

More complete input means deeper AI reasoning, which usually means more Credits consumed.

---

## Free Credits for New Users

When a new user registers an account, they receive **100 free Credits**, which is enough to experience Treeify's full workflow.
